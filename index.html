Gomoku is easy to play. Players take turns to place black and white pieces on a grid board. Black always starts first. To win, place 5 pieces of the same colour in a row. These pieces can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal. That's it!

Board Size:
Choose between playing on a 15 by 15 grid or 19 by 19 board

Game Rules:
In Free play, players simply take turns to put their pieces on any grid throughout the game
In Tournament, the black player starts off by placing 2 black pieces and 1 white piece. The white player, then, chooses to either;
1. Continue as white
2. Swap to black
3. Place 1 white and 1 black piece and let black decide to continue or swap
